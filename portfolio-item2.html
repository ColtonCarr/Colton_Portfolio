AI Movement

Breathing life into pieces

In this project, I was tasked with setting up an AI using C# and Unity's NavMesh feature. The AI is coded to move from NavPoint to NavPoint unless a player piece enters the AI's line of vision. Once the player is spotted, the AI will then move to a collider and trip the alarm.

This project was designed to get us intoduced to state machines and learning how that can be used to create an opposing force
